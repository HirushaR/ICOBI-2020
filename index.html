WHAT IS ICOBI

International Conference on Business Innovation 2020 (ICOBI 2020) is the third consecutive international research conference that will be organized and held at NSBM Green University Town of Sri Lanka. ICOBI 2018 was held on 25th August 2018 under the theme “Business Resurgence and Green Living” and ICOBI 2019 was held on 22nd November 2019 under the theme “Reviving Talent and Green Living”.

NSBM Journal On Business Management

All authors who present their papers at the conference will be invited to submit an extended version on their research paper to the NSBM Journal on Business Management – Print ISSN: 2465-5643. E periodical: TBA. All submitted papers will go through a blind review process for acceptance and will be published on a digital platform without an additional cost.

ICOBI 2020 Conference Proceedings

All accepted and presented papers at the conference (ICOBI 2020) will be published in the conference proceedings (Print ISSN: TBA. E-Periodical ISSN: TBA).
